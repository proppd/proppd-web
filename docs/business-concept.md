# PROPPD

## Business Concept & Business Model

## Executive Summary

Proppd is a South African prop-tech platform designed to modernise the real estate industry through fair pricing, verified leads, AI-powered tools, and a modern property marketplace.

The platform begins as a consumer-facing property portal where buyers, renters, landlords, sellers, and agents connect through real listings and verified enquiries.

Over time, Proppd evolves into a complete operating system for estate agents and agencies, offering CRM tools, lead verification, AI automation, WhatsApp communication, marketing tools, workflow management, and transaction infrastructure.

Proppd exists to solve growing frustration in the real estate industry around expensive listing portals, fake leads, outdated software, fragmented workflows, poor user experience, and low transparency.

## Mission

To build the fairest and most intelligent property platform in South Africa.

## Vision

To become the infrastructure layer for modern real estate across Africa.

## Brand Positioning

The modern property platform built for agents and consumers — not portal exploitation.

Proppd is designed around transparency, fairness, technology, automation, trust, and modern UX.

## The Problem

The South African real estate industry is heavily dependent on expensive property portals and disconnected software systems.

Agents and agencies face major challenges:

- Expensive listing platforms that force agents to pay large monthly fees simply to compete for visibility.
- Fake, duplicated, spam, and unqualified enquiries that waste time and money.
- Outdated real estate software that is difficult to use, not mobile-first, lacks automation, does not integrate properly, and is not AI-enabled.
- Fragmented workflows across listings, leads, communication, social media, documents, and reporting.
- Poor consumer experience through inaccurate listings, outdated listings, slow responses, poor search, and low transparency.

## The Solution

Proppd is building a modern property ecosystem that combines a property portal, verified lead infrastructure, agent tools, AI automation, communication systems, and real estate workflow technology.

The first phase focuses on a high-quality property marketplace with clean UX, verified enquiries, fast search, modern mobile design, and fair access for agents.

## Product Ecosystem

### Phase 1 — Property Portal

A consumer marketplace for buying property, renting property, browsing agents, connecting with agencies, and submitting verified enquiries.

Core features:

- Listings
- Advanced search
- Agent profiles
- Agency profiles
- Enquiry management
- Lead verification
- Mobile-first UX

### Phase 2 — Agent Tools

A modern operating system for estate agents.

Features:

- CRM
- Lead tracking
- AI listing descriptions
- Automated follow-ups
- WhatsApp integration
- Social media generation
- Seller reports
- Task management
- Performance analytics

### Phase 3 — AI Infrastructure

An AI-powered automation layer for real estate professionals.

Features:

- AI lead qualification
- AI communication assistant
- AI property matching
- AI marketing generation
- AI valuation assistance
- Automated reporting
- Workflow automation

## Target Market

Primary market: estate agents and agencies looking for better exposure, better leads, modern tools, and fairer pricing.

Secondary market: property seekers looking for trustworthy listings, easy search, verified agents, and faster communication.

Future markets include developers, landlords, commercial brokers, mortgage originators, attorneys, insurers, and property investors.

## Competitive Advantage

1. Verified leads that reduce fake enquiries, spam, duplicate leads, and low-intent users.
2. Fair pricing without exploitative subscription models.
3. AI-native architecture for automation, smart lead scoring, and intelligent property matching.
4. WhatsApp-first communication for the South African market.
5. Modern UX focused on speed, simplicity, mobile-first design, and intuitive workflows.

## Revenue Model

### Phase 1 Revenue

- Featured listings: promoted listings, homepage exposure, premium search positioning.
- Agency packages: monthly subscriptions for additional listings, branding, analytics, and team accounts.

### Phase 2 Revenue

- AgentOS subscription: CRM, AI tools, automation, reporting, and communication.
- Lead verification services: premium lead protection and verification tools.

### Phase 3 Revenue

- AI subscription products: assistants, automation, marketing generation, workflow optimisation.
- Marketplace partnerships: referral revenue from bond originators, attorneys, insurers, moving companies, and utilities.

## Growth Strategy

1. Build listings inventory by onboarding agents and agencies.
2. Grow consumer traffic through SEO, social media, property content, neighbourhood pages, and search visibility.
3. Introduce agent tools including CRM, automation, AI tools, and lead management.
4. Expand into mortgage services, transactions, AI workflows, analytics, and market intelligence.

## Technology Philosophy

Proppd should be cloud-native, API-driven, mobile-first, AI-ready, scalable, and automation-friendly.

Recommended architecture:

- React / Next.js frontend
- Node.js or Laravel backend
- PostgreSQL database
- Cloud storage/CDN
- Modern search infrastructure

## Brand Philosophy

The brand should feel modern, trustworthy, intelligent, clean, premium, and disruptive.

It should not feel corporate-heavy, old-fashioned, cluttered, or luxury cliché.

## Long-Term Opportunity

The long-term opportunity is not just becoming another listing portal.

The opportunity is becoming the operating system for African real estate across listings, leads, communication, transactions, automation, AI infrastructure, agent productivity, and property intelligence.

## Conclusion

Proppd is building a modern alternative to traditional property portals by combining trusted listings, verified leads, modern UX, fair pricing, and AI-enabled infrastructure.

The company begins with a property portal designed to attract consumers and agents, then evolves into a complete technology ecosystem powering the future of real estate in South Africa and beyond.

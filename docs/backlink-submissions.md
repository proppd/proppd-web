Proppd is a South African online property portal connecting buyers and tenants with PPRA-verified estate agents. Browse verified listings for sale and to rent, request property valuations, and contact accredited agents directly. Every agent on Proppd holds a valid Fidelity Fund Certificate, verified live against the PPRA register during onboarding. The platform differentiates itself from incumbent portals by prioritizing listing quality, agent verification, and transparent mandate information. Free for property seekers; agents can sign up at proppd.com/for-agents.

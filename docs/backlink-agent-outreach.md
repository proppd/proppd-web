# Agent Outreach Email — Backlink Request

> Send to agencies already listed on Proppd. Personalise the [bracketed] fields.
> These backlinks are the highest value because they come from relevant SA
> property websites with context.

---

## Subject line options (pick one)

1. Quick favour — link to your Proppd profile from your site?
2. [Agency Name] on Proppd — mutual visibility
3. Can we get a "Listed on Proppd" link on your site?

---

## Email template

Hi [Agent First Name],

Hope you're doing well — [Agency Name] has been live on Proppd and your listings are
already getting enquiries from buyers in [their area].

I have a quick favour that helps us both: would you be able to add a link to your
Proppd profile from your agency website? Something like "Listed on Proppd" or
"Find us on Proppd" in your footer, contact page, or "Where we advertise" section.

**Your profile link:** https://proppd.com/agencies/[their-slug]

It helps your clients verify you're PPRA-accredited, improves your Proppd profile
authority, and tells other agents in [their area] that Proppd is where the action is.

If you're able to, something like this works:

```html
<a href="https://proppd.com/agencies/[their-slug]">Listed on Proppd</a>
```

Happy to feature [Agency Name] in our next "Agent Spotlight" on socials as a
thank you. Let me know either way!

Best,
Voxi
Proppd
info@proppd.com

---

## Who to target first

Prioritise agencies that:
- Already have active listings on Proppd (they have skin in the game)
- Have their own websites (not just Facebook pages)
- Are in competitive areas (Sandton, Cape Town, Durban) — more link equity
- Mention "PPRA" or "FFC" on their site already — natural fit

---

## Follow-up sequence

1. **Day 0:** Send initial email
2. **Day 5:** If no response, send: "Just checking if you saw this — happy to
   send a pre-written blurb you can paste straight into your site."
3. **Day 12:** Final follow-up with a screenshot of their Proppd profile showing
   the verified badge, reinforcing the value of linking to it.
